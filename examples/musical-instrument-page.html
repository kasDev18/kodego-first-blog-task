Music from My Heart.

Playing Musical Instruments

A passion that is bloom from a very young age would not be forgotten. You can be busy and not able to do it as much as before, but your soul will definitely find a way to atleast reminisce the the joy it brings to you.

"Music gives me so much happiness. I appreciated all kinds of music whether its latest, oldies, rock or classical. Even the sound of nature, I really appreciated it."

-KAS

So how this passion of me start?

My Passion for music starts when I am in grade school. I used to be a guitarist, but when I become a part of groups in schools and in outside, I became more eager to learn a lot of musical instruments. I am not the kind that disregard other not so cool instruments like tambourine, eggshaker, bar chimes, etc. because of its style. I can even accept role as a lyrist in lyre band. That's how I am crazy about playing musical instruments.

Bajo is the type of musical instrument that I believe quite challenging for me. It is very heavy yet deepest vibration. The error when playing this is very obvious, so you need to have a great focus when playing it. It also has no frets(like on guitar) that serve as traceability to know where to tap the string. But despite of the complicated situtaion of the Bajo, I am always wanting to play it in audience. I'm just not able to do it when I was in college because our maestro always placed me in percussion role as they say that I am more beautiful in this way .

I feel so sad that I am not able to have time for it because of my schoolung and work at the same time. But still,I always planning to have a band set someday and also I am enjoying planning my soon-to-be schedule for my instruments.
